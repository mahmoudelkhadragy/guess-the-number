About The Game

Guess The Number

It is an easy game just for fun and try to guess the number from 1 to 100.
You have to guess it with littel number of tries.
If you guess the wrong number it will display an alert with usefull message that you will know if your guessed number it is high or low from the correct number. There are to levels Easy and Hard, Easy level will let you see the history as you can remmeber the last range numbers you tried to enter, Hard level without history and you have to remmeber the range number you entered to guess the correct number.
